Find ideal customer leads for Cashbakery. Focus primarily on fitness and wellness businesses, followed by beauty and personal care. Target brick-and-mortar SMBs doing $1M+ in annual revenue, ideally with 2+ locations.
Each lead should contain:
- Company/Business Name
- If it's a franchise or multi-location business, include the parent brand name
- Geographical Location
- Industry/Sub-industry
- Company/Business website URL
- Estimated number of locations (if available)
- A small description of the company/business
- The Person to Contact
- Their Role/Title
- Their Email
- A small summary as to why you consider this person is the right contact for this company/business
- Redact a personalized outreach Email using relevant company/business information with the goal of getting them to schedule a call with the founders of Cashbakery. You can use the email_examples.md file for examples on how we currently do it.

For fitness and wellness, include leads from: gym chains and boutique fitness studios (CrossFit, F45, Orangetheory, independent studios), yoga and pilates studios (CorePower, Club Pilates, independent multi-location studios), martial arts and boxing gyms, personal training studios, dance studios, wellness centers, and med spas. Include at least 2 leads per business for multi-location operators.
For beauty and personal care, include leads from: salon chains and multi-location salons, spas and day spas, barbershop chains, nail salon groups, med spas and aesthetic clinics, and tanning studios. Focus on businesses with 2+ locations or high-revenue single locations.
You can generate multiple leads for the same company/business if you find multiple persons that fit the criteria, just be sure to order them so all leads for the same company/business appear together.
Each individual lead MUST have an email, this is non-negotiable as the outreach is going to be done through email.
Save all leads in a structured CSV file inside the /leads directory.
