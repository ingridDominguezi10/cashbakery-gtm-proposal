## Our Product

Our product is **Cashbakery** - a financial intelligence platform that gives brick-and-mortar business owners real-time visibility into their cash flow across all locations, bank accounts, and revenue streams. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, multiple bank logins, and disconnected accounting tools, Cashbakery unifies everything into one dashboard with AI-powered forecasting. Owners can connect their bank accounts (via Plaid), accounting software (like QuickBooks), and POS systems (like MarianaTek) to get a unified view of inflows, outflows, recurring transactions, and projected cash position across 7 days to 12 months. The platform supports multi-location businesses, custom categorization, transaction rules, and exportable reports - all designed for operators who need to make fast financial decisions without an accounting degree.
