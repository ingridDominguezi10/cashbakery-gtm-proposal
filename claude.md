## Our Product

Our product is called **Nix Pro**. It is a group-monitoring app from Nix Biosensors, a single administrator - coach, nutritionist, safety supervisor, or researcher - can manage the hydration strategies for multiple subjects at once, both in real time and with post-session data summaries and sweat profiles. To keep the team, unit or work shift properly hydrated, you can’t just use a “one size fits all” approach. You need to craft custom strategies informed by each individual’s personal biology. Yet this data has been nearly impossible to access up until now. Nix Pro empowers you to deliver optimized safety, performance, and efficiency for all members of your squad. Nix Pro is empowering admins to access and understand the unique hydration needs of each of their subjects. Each session can be customized to the type of activity, expected intensity, weight of equipment, and more. Additional environmental factors, including temperature, humidity, and altitude can be layered into the group analysis.
